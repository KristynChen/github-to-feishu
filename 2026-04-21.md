## 🧪 邏輯對抗專題：幾何崩潰點測試 (Sequential vs. Flat)

為了驗證 `build_sequence` 的真實價值，我們進行了四組對應測試。我們發現生圖模型在面對簡單物體時具備補償能力，但在複雜幾何下，缺乏順序會導致 **「幾何邏輯坍縮」**。

### Case 0: 基礎零件 (中性測試)
- **場景**：簡單的鋁製支架與底座。
- **結果**：兩者視覺上差異不大，視覺模型可自行校正。
- **證據**：[有序版](case_0_seq.png) | [扁平版](case_0_flat.png)

### Case 1: 巢狀布爾 (Nested Boolean)
- **場景**：大方塊內套圓柱，垂直穿孔。
- **結果**：有序版生成的剖視圖邏輯嚴密；扁平版雖然視覺呈現尚可，但空間層次感缺失。
- **證據**：[有序版](case_1_seq.png) | [扁平版](case_1_flat.png)

### Case 2: 特徵依附 (Dependency Case) 🔴 **CRITICAL FAILURE**
- **場景**：膠囊體結合處先倒角，後在倒角曲面上切槽。
- **結果**：**扁平版徹底潰敗**。模型因無法理解倒角與切槽的順序，生成了完全不對稱且邏輯混亂的幾何怪物。
- **結論**：對於具備拓撲依賴的特徵，`build_sequence` 是成功的唯一保證。
- **證據**：[有序版](case_2_seq.png) | [扁平版](case_2_flat.png)

### Case 3: 布爾鍊 (Mathematical Chain)
- **場景**：立方體 (A - B) + C - D 的複雜集合運算。
- **結果**：有序版給出了完美的加工程序圖；扁平版則呈現出隨機特徵堆砌的「視覺拼貼」，不具備工程加工特徵。
- **證據**：[有序版](case_3_seq.png) | [扁平版](case_3_flat.png)

### Case 1: 巢狀布爾 (Nested Boolean)

Isometric view, high precision, matte gray plastic, clear internal cross-section detail.`
- **Flat B**: `A professional CAD technical rendering of a 100mm cube. It includes these components: a 60mm square cutout, a 40mm diameter internal cylinder, a 100mm base cube, and a 10mm circular through-hole. Isometric view, precision engineering style, matte gray plastic material.`

### Case 2: 特徵依附 (Dependency Case)
- **Sequential A**: `A professional CAD technical rendering. Sequence: 1. A vertical cylinder joined with a sphere on top (capsule shape). 2. A 10mm radius fillet is applied to the circular intersection edge where the cylinder meets the sphere, creating a smooth transition. 3. - **Flat B**: `A professional CAD technical rendering of a capsule shape (cylinder joined with a sphere). Features include: a rectangular horizontal slot on the surface, a vertical cylinder and top sphere base, and a 10mm radius fillet on the circular intersection edge. Isometric view, technical drawing style, matte gray plastic material. Clear engineering labels.`

### Case 3: 布爾鍊 (Mathematical Chain)
- **Sequential A**: `A professional CAD technical rendering. Sequence: 1. A 100mm base cube. 2. A 50mm cylinder is SUBTRACTED from the top-left corner. 3. Isometric view, blueprint style, matte gray.`
- **Flat B**: `A professional CAD technical rendering of a composite body. Its features include: a tiny 5mm circular through-hole, a 50mm cylinder subtracted from a corner, a 40mm sphere merged with a corner, and a 100mm base cube. Isometric view, technical blueprint style, matte gray plastic material.`
